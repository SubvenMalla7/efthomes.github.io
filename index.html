About EFT

EFT stands for Emotional Freedom Technique. It is a therapy technique where an individual taps at different acupuncture points in the body while recalling positive affirmations, thus being able to bring a positive healing power in the body. This technique can be used in a varied circumstances in a person's life, ranging from sleep disorders, to quitting smoking, to mental health problems, to improving one's habit, to feeling positive every day, or other simple to complex problems.

EFT is such a technique which has no side effects, needs no medications, and can be used in any circumstance after learning it once. EFT helps in activating the inner energy of our body and rejuvenating the healing powers from within. So, it is a one-time investment to instill a better habit for life time. Meet Your Trainer
